FIRST AID
WAYS THAT MAY HELP TO SAVE LIVES

What should we do?

  • Call emergency first and stay calm, don't panic.
  • Have water, or consume chocolate since dark chocolate is high in flavonoids, a subtype called flavanols that is associated with a lower risk of heart disease
  • Be comfortable sit down or lean against a chair or a wall.
  • While waiting for an emergency can take nitroglecerin or some medicine as prescribed by doctor.
  • Push hard and fast on the chest of a person in a fairly rapid rythm.
  • Monitor the symptoms.
